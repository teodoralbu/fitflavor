Change the PageContainer className from `"py-12"` to `"pt-12 pb-24"` on line 143. This gives 96px bottom padding on the content, ensuring the "Sign out" button and all content is well above the bottom nav. The `pt-12` preserves the existing top padding (48px). `pb-24` = 96px in Tailwind.

